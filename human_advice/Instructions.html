Overview

You are participating in a decision-making experiment. You will report your beliefs about certain things. You will answer 16 questions by allocating 100 tokens for each question across a set of possible answers Placing more tokens in a specific bin means you are more confident the answer falls in that range.

Interface to report Beliefs

For each question, you will give a report about what you believe to be the correct answer. You will use a slider interface like this below to allocate {{ num_tokens }} tokens across a set of possible outcomes. Each interface has 10 bins.

Payment

You will receive a $40 participation payment for agreeing to participate in this experiment, which will be paid in cash at the end of the session.

Lab Rules

Please put your cell phones on silent and securely put away all phones, laptops and other electronic devices. Please do not talk with others seated nearby for the duration of the session. If at any point you have a question, please raise your hand and a researcher will come to assist you.
