Consent to Participate in Research

You are invited to participate in a research study about decision-making and judgments under uncertainty. Please read the information below carefully before deciding whether to participate.

Purpose of the Study

The purpose of this study is to better understand how individuals make decisions and evaluate information with augmented decision-making tools or cognitive scaffolds.

What You Will Do

If you agree to participate, you will complete some decision-making tasks and answer related questions. Participation is expected to take approximately 15–30 minutes.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is completely voluntary. You may stop participating at any time without penalty by closing your browser window.

Risks and Discomforts

This study involves minimal risk, no greater than risks encountered in everyday life.

Benefits

You may not receive any direct benefit from participating. However, your participation may help researchers better understand decision-making behavior, especially as it relates to using algorithmic or AI tools.

Compensation

You will receive compensation according to the payment rules described in the experiment, including any participation payment and earnings from tasks.

Confidentiality

Your responses will be kept confidential to the extent permitted by law. No personally identifying information will be reported in research findings.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this study, please contact the research team.

Consent Statement

By clicking “Next,” you confirm that:

  • You are at least 18 years old or otherwise eligible to participate.
  • You have read and understood this information.
  • You voluntarily agree to participate in this research study.
