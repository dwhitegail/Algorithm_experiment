Overview

You are participating in a decision-making experiment. Your earnings will depend on the beliefs you report across four tasks: a weight estimation task, a height estimation task, an urns task, and a song ranking task. The weight and height tasks each contain one question; the urns and song ranking tasks each contain two questions, for a total of six questions.

You will answer each question by allocating tokens across a set of possible answers. AT THE START OF THE SESSION, you will RANDOMLY be assigned to receive advice from one of three groups: Algorithmic Advice or Human Advice, or No Advice.

There are 2 rounds for each question. Each question will start with round 1, after which you may or may not purchase and receive advice. After which you can proceed to round 2. Some tasks feature multiple questions; in those cases, you will complete round 1 for all questions in that task before moving on.

Round 1 - Initial or Pre-Beliefs

For each question, you will give a report of your initial belief about the correct answer. You will use a slider interface like this below to allocate {{ num_tokens }} tokens across a set of possible outcomes. Each interface has 10 bins.

Placing more tokens in a specific bin means you are more confident the answer falls in that range. Your accuracy determines the probability of winning $25 for each report. Therefore, your best strategy to maximize your earnings is to always allocate your tokens honestly according to your true beliefs.

Advice Phase

After completing Round 1, you will have the opportunity to purchase advice before making your final decision. You can only purchase advice if you were assigned to the Algorithmic or Human advice conditions. You will be shown a price list with varying costs for advice. You must make a choice in every row about whether you want to buy the advice at that specific price.

If you purchase advice, the cost will be deducted from your $10.00 advice fund, called an endowment. If you are in the No Advice group, you will simply wait for the next round to begin.

Round 2 - Final or Post-beliefs

After the advice phase, you will have the opportunity to update your beliefs. You will once again allocate {{ num_tokens }} tokens across 10 bins. You may change your token allocation based on the advice you received, or you may keep it exactly the same.

Payment

Your total payment consists of three parts:

  • Participation Fee: You have already earned ${{ participation_fee }} for agreeing to participate in this experiment, which will be paid in cash at the end of the session.
  • Endowment: You are given a ${{ endowment }} endowment to cover the cost of purchasing advice across all 4 tasks. Any money left over in from your endowment at the end of the experiment is yours to keep.
  • Task Earnings: At the end of the experiment, we will look at how accurately you allocated your tokens. Based on your score, you can earn up to $25 per report. Remember, however, that only one task will randomly be selected for payment. Depending on the task chosen, you can earn a maximum of $50 to $100.

Lab Rules

Please put your cell phones on silent and securely put away all phones, laptops and other electronic devices. Please do not talk with others seated nearby for the duration of the session. If at any point you have a question, please raise your hand and a researcher will come to assist you.
