Overview

Welcome! In this experiment you will be asked to report your beliefs by answering a series of questions. You will do so by allocating tokens for each task across a set of possible answers.

Pre-beliefs

For each question, you will give an initial report of your belief about the correct answer using a belief elicitation interface to allocate {{ num_tokens }} tokens across 10 bins. More tokens in a bin means you are more confident the answer falls there. Your score determines the probability of winning ${{ max_earnings }} for each report. The best strategy is always to allocate tokens honestly according to your true beliefs.

Advice

After completing the initial round for each question you may choose to purchase advice. You will be randomly assigned to receive algorithmic or human advice. The cost of advice will be deducted from your endowment.

Post-beliefs

After receiving advice, you can update your report about your belief of the correct answer to again allocate {{ num_tokens }} tokens across 10 bins.

Payment

Your payment consists of three parts:

  • Participation Fee: ${{ participation_fee }} — paid to everyone regardless of performance.
  • Endowment: ${{ endowment }} — given at the start. Reduced each time you purchase advice.
  • Task Earnings: Based on how well you allocate your tokens you can earn up to ${{ max_earnings }} per report.
