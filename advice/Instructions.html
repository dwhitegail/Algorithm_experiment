Overview

Welcome! In this experiment you will be asked to estimate answers to a series of questions by allocating tokens across a set of possible answers.

Your Tokens

You will be given {{ num_tokens }} tokens per question. Allocate them across the bins to reflect your belief about the correct answer. More tokens in a bin means you are more confident the answer falls there.

Advice

After completing the initial round for each question you may choose to purchase advice from a Multiple Price List. The cost of advice will be deducted from your endowment.

Payment

Your payment consists of three parts:

  • Participation Fee: ${{ participation_fee }} — paid to everyone regardless of performance.
  • Endowment: ${{ endowment }} — given at the start. Reduced each time you purchase advice.
  • Task Earnings: Based on how well you allocate your tokens you can earn up to ${{ max_earnings }} per question.

Scoring Rule

Your token allocation is scored using a quadratic scoring rule combined with a binary lottery. Your score determines the probability of winning ${{ max_earnings }} for that question. The best strategy is always to allocate tokens honestly according to your true beliefs.
